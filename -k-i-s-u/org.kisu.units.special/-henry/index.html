Henry

class Henry : Scalar<Metric, Henry>

Represents the SI derived unit of inductance: henry (H).

One henry is the inductance of a closed circuit in which an electromotive force of one volt is produced when the electric current changes uniformly at one ampere per second.

SI definition: H = m²·kg·s⁻²·A⁻².
