Product

class Product<A : Expression<A>, System<A>, B : Expression<B>, System<B>>(left: A, right: B) : Expression<Product<A, B>> , System<Product<A, B>>

Represents the product of two unit expressions in the physical unit system.

This class is part of the unit expression framework for modeling complex physical units such as N·m (newton-meter), mol·K, or W·s, by combining simpler unit components through multiplication.

A Product composes two expressions — left and right — which are themselves both Expressions and Systems. The resulting expression retains type safety and can be further composed, multiplied, or divided with other units to build arbitrarily complex units.

For example:

  • Newton * Meter becomes Product<Newton, Meter> and represents N·m

  • Mole * Kelvin becomes mol·K

  • You can further multiply or divide these to construct expressions like kg·m/s²

the type of the left component (e.g., Newton, Mole), must implement Expression and System
